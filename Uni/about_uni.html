About Us

Welcome to the Uni Resource Portal! Our mission is to provide a comprehensive platform where students from First-Year (FY), Second-Year (SY), and Third-Year (TY) can easily access and share educational resources such as syllabi, PYQs ,assignments, and notes.

This portal was created to help students succeed by offering a centralized location for valuable academic materials. Whether you need to catch up on class notes, find the latest assignments, or review the syllabus for your courses, we've got you covered.

Our Goals

  • Foster a community of sharing and collaboration among students.
  • Provide easy and organized access to academic resources.
  • Support students in their educational journey by offering high-quality materials.

Meet the Team

Our team consists of dedicated educators and students who understand the challenges of academic life. We are committed to continuously improving this platform to meet your needs.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us. We would love to hear from you!
